# gtm-lead-routing-pipeline
Zapier GTM automation — HubSpot to Salesforce to Slack

GTM Lead Routing & CRM Sync Pipeline
Tools: Zapier · HubSpot · Salesforce · Slack

The Problem
Sales team had 4-hour lead response times. Leads came into HubSpot but weren't synced to Salesforce. SDRs assigned manually. Duplicate records everywhere.

The Solution
Built a multi-step Zapier workflow triggered on every new HubSpot lead:

Step 1: Enrich lead with firmographic data
Step 2: Score lead — hot or cold
Step 3: Sync to Salesforce — deduplicated
Step 4: Route hot leads to correct SDR via Slack
Step 5: Cold leads go to nurture sequence automatically
Step 6: Log all activity in both HubSpot and Salesforce

## Results
- ⚡ Lead response: 4 hours → 5 minutes
- 🔁 Duplicate records: Eliminated
- 👤 Manual SDR assignment: Zero
- ⏰ RevOps hours saved: 6+ per week

Tools Used
Zapier — workflow automation and routing
HubSpot — lead source and nurture sequences
Salesforce — CRM record creation and logging
Slack — real-time SDR notification
